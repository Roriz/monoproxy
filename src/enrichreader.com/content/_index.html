Automated Multi-Stage Ingestion Pipeline

We bypass manual, slow metadata compilation. Our engine parses massive prose volumes automatically to build fully trackable databases.

Massive Narrative Scaling

The engine runs dozens of specialized, continuously evolving heuristics steps directly on the full text. This extracts megabytes of relationship metadata per chapter—generating data footprints far larger than the chapter prose itself.

Human Macro-Validation (Non-Blocking)

While the engine generates data fully automatically, we double-check structural macro-patterns post-parse with narrative analysts. This non-blocking step identifies anomalies and serves as a feedback loop to continuously refine and evolve our parsing heuristics week over week.

1

Multi-Pass Entity Clustering

The engine runs name discovery passes across the entire series, resolving aliases, pronouns, and grouping mentions to track characters, groups, items, and locations.

2

Temporal Page-by-Page Tracking

Our parser sweeps page-by-page to monitor timeline offsets, character presence gaps (absence spans), and status changes (e.g. character mortality or species switches).

3

Accountable Attribute Trackback

Every extracted metric or character tag is mapped directly back to the exact passage and chapter of the source text. Users and platforms can trace any data point back to its literal context, ensuring absolute integrity and verification.

Technology & FAQ

Technical answers regarding our automated parsing engine, B2B APIs, and data loops.

Our custom narrative engine processes books page-by-page through dozens of specialized, continuously evolving heuristics steps. Instead of generic AI prompts, it runs mathematical and syntax parsing algorithms. As a post-parse, non-blocking step, humans review macro-level patterns to flag discrepancies. This feedback loop, combined with thousands of granular corrections submitted by readers using public skins, allows us to continuously evolve and refine our extraction pipeline week over week.
Platforms (like web novel apps) can access our structured database via REST APIs to dynamically populate in-app character panels and lore indexes. Adaptation studios and publishers can receive structured JSON catalog exports and continuity reports.
The engine is optimized specifically for massive stories. It easily handles 1,000+ chapter serialization series (e.g. 2M+ words) where manual structural tracking becomes impossible for human editors.
