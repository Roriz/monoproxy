How EnrichReader Works

Three steps to turn your static books into a living, interactive world. No lock-in, just better reading.

1

100% Local

Bring your own files. The EnrichReader parsing engine runs entirely on your device. We never upload your books or track them on a server. Your reading stays completely private.

2

The Skin

Search and install a Skin. Skins add interactivity, lore lookup, and memory to your books.

3

The Experience

Open your book to read a living story. The reader updates character bios and timeline events as you turn the pages.

Beyond Static Pages
Our Vision

Character Profile
