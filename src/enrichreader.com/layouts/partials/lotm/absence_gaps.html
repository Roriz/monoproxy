Narrative Absence & Perspective Analysis

The Perspective Anchors

Klein Moretti is omitted from this timeline because he has maximum disappearance gaps of only 15 chapters (during his rebirth). As the central narrative engine and protagonist, his near-constant presence forms the structural spine of the text.

The Mysterious Patron (Azik)

Azik Eggers vanishes for a staggering 630 chapters (Ch. 210 to 840). This corresponds to his long slumber to recover his memories and his travel to find his past, before re-appearing to protect Klein in critical battles.

Divergent Pathways

Key allies like Leonard Mitchell (700 ch. gap) and Sharron (660 ch. gap) have significant absences. Because allies operate in different countries and organizations, they vanish from the core perspective until their paths intersect or they gather at the Sefirah Castle.
