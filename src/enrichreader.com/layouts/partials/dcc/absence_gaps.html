Narrative Absence & Perspective Analysis

The Perspective Anchors

Carl and Donut have maximum disappearance gaps of only 4 and 8 chapters respectively. As the central narrative engines, their near-constant presence forms the structural spine of the text, with gaps occurring only during solo trials or cutscenes.

The Demotion and Return (Zev)

Zev holds the record for the largest absence, vanishing for a staggering 285 chapters (Ch. 82 to 367). This corresponds to her early syndicate demotion/removal as manager and her eventual re-appearance as part of the underground rebellion.

Divergent Dungeon Paths

Crawlers like Prepotente (165 ch. gap) and managers like Juice Box (188 ch. gap) have massive absences. Because crawlers traverse separate floor zones, they vanish from the core perspective until paths merge at central events.
