The Early Floor Filter (Volume 1)

By the end of Volume 1, 26 characters have died out of 120 introduced (a 21.6% initial mortality rate). Early floors serve as a rapid filter, quickly eliminating secondary and tertiary characters introduced in the panic of the initial collapse.

Steady Attrition to Plunge

While the survival rate hovers steadily around 70% through Volume 6, it takes a sharp plunge to 62.1% in Volume 7. This indicates that while intermediate floors have manageable attrition, transitioning into open system warfare collapses the safety margin.

The 9th Floor Massacre (Peak Lethality)

Volume 7, Chapter 13 is the single most lethal chapter in the series, recording 5 key character deaths in a single sequence. This climactic battlefield clash of the Faction War represents the absolute peak of system lethality.
